Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dayjour?

Dayjour is an advanced data analysis platform that uses Secure Multi-Party Computation (SMPC) to help financial institutions detect money laundering and fraud by securely analyzing encrypted data without exposing sensitive information.

How does Dayjour ensure data security?

Dayjour utilizes SMPC and encryption techniques, ensuring that sensitive financial data remains secure while still allowing multiple parties to collaboratively perform analysis and detect fraudulent activity.

Can Dayjour integrate with my financial systems?

Yes, Dayjour can integrate with a variety of financial systems and data sources to streamline analysis and provide real-time fraud detection and compliance checks.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, Dayjour offers a free trial that allows you to explore its features, including secure data analysis and fraud detection capabilities, before committing to a subscription.

What types of support are available for Dayjour users?

We offer comprehensive support through various channels including email, phone, and live chat. Additionally, Dayjour provides documentation and tutorials to assist you with setup and operation.

How can I contact Dayjour's customer support team?

You can reach out to our customer support team via email at support@dayjour.com, or through our contact form available on the website.
